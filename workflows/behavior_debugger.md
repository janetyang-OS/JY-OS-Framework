## âš™ï¸ How to Use This Tool
This is your **Action Menu** when things go wrong. Do not react emotionally; follow the columns.

### Column 1: The User Bug Report (Parent Complaint)
* **What you say:** "He keeps asking for examples!" / "He is procrastinating!"
* **Engineering View:** This is just a symptom. Do not treat the symptom.

### Column 2: The Diagnosis (Engineering Translation)
* **The Shift:** We reframe moral judgments into system faults.
    * "Lazy" $\rightarrow$ **Task Friction too high**.
    * "Copying" $\rightarrow$ **Cognitive Ownership Leak**.
    * "Drama" $\rightarrow$ **Emotional Noise Contamination**.
* **Why:** You can't fix "Laziness." You **can** fix "Friction."

### Column 3: The Constraint Rule (The AI Coach Configuration)
* **The Fix:** What rule do we enforce in the Kernel?
    * *Leak:* **No Examples + Ask-Back Protocol.**
    * *No Boundary:* **Sentence Limit + Hard Stop.**
    * *Panic:* **Facts-Only Mode.**

### Column 4: Metrics to Watch (Telemetry)
* **The Validation:** How do we know it worked?
    * Watch the **Dependency Frequency**.
    * Watch the **Self-Correction Rate**.
    * Watch the **Emotional Noise Level**.

### Column 5: The Tuning Knob (Action)
* **The Governance:**
    * **Increase refusal strictness.**
    * **Tighten limits.**
    * **Lower difficulty.**
    * **Switch Modes (Practice $\leftrightarrow$ Strict).**

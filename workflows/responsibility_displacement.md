## üèó The 3 Structural Components

### 1. The Input: Parental Intervention Level (Left)
* **Low:** Trust & Autonomy.
* **High:** Micromanagement & Excuses.
* **Note:** This scale does not judge *intention* (which is usually love), only *intensity*.

### 2. The Buffer: The Displacement Zone (Center)
* **What forms here?** Excuses, Mediation, Noise.
* **Function:** This zone "catches" the responsibility that the child was supposed to hold.
* **The Cost:** It creates a "Safety Net" that prevents the child from feeling the weight of their choices.

### 3. The Output: Child Responsibility Ownership (Right)
* **High:** Self-Driven, Problem Solving.
* **Low:** Passive, Dependent.
* **The Law:** As the parent lifts the weight (Left goes up), the child's muscle atrophies (Right goes down).

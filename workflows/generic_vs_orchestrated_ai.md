## üó£ Left Side: Generic AI (The Chatbot Trap)
**Structure:** Hub-and-Spoke (Central Bot, Many Users).
**Characteristics:**
* **"Ask Anything":** No defined scope.
* **"Depends on how you ask":** Quality is 100% dependent on the user's prompting skill.
* **"User decides next step":** The AI stops after every reply. The human must carry the mental load of "What do I do next?"

**Result:** High Cognitive Load. The user is the Engine; the AI is just the Fuel.

---

## ‚öôÔ∏è Right Side: Orchestrated AI (The System)
**Structure:** Pipeline / Assembly Line.
**Characteristics:**
* **Roles Defined:** The AI is not "ChatGPT"; it is an "Orchestrator," a "Checker," or a "Coach."
* **Flow Designed:** The output of the Orchestrator becomes the input of the Coach.
* **Responsibility Assigned:** The "Checker" owns the accuracy; the "Executor" owns the output.

**The Workflow:**
1.  **Orchestrator:** Breaks down the mission.
2.  **Checker/Coach:** Validates logic (Quality Control).
3.  **Executor:** Produces the draft.
4.  **Observer:** Monitors the process (Metadata).

**Result:** Low Friction. The System is the Engine; the human is the Pilot.

**The difference is not the model (GPT-4 vs. Claude 3).**
The difference is **Structure.**
* **Generic:** Responsibility stays on the User.
* **Orchestrated:** Responsibility is distributed into the Architecture.
